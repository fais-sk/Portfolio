Flagship Implementations

Six problems, six platforms

Each one is a different shape of the same question: how do you get a business's physical operations to move as fast as its data does.

WAYPOINT · JBA, UAE

Nine GPS Systems, One Border Crossing

Implemented TMS & Control Tower — inter-country GCC movement

FedEx needed to see a truck the moment it crossed a border, not the moment it arrived. I implemented TMS and Control Tower for inter-country movement out of Jebel Ali, integrating 9 separate GPS systems into one view, and built custom geofences — Al Batha, Sila, Hatta Wajajah — capturing vehicle in/out times for customs clearance across the GCC. Planned route times (JBA–RUH, for example) were benchmarked live against actual GPS data, lifting fulfilment accuracy and earning Employee of the Month, July 2022.

WAYPOINT · MCT, OMAN

Four Divisions, One Route Plan

Implemented TMS + Control Tower — P&G, BEL, CPG, Commodity divisions

Khimji Ramdas ran four divisions on different logistics logic across Muscat, Sohar, Sur, and Nizwa. I implemented TMS integrated with SAP for sales-order ingestion, automated trip sheets, and built Route & Load Plans that worked across all four divisions on one platform, with Control Tower running on mobile-device GPS hotspots. Result: 20% logistics cost reduction and a "Project of the Year" award.

WAYPOINT · COK, INDIA

From SAP Hybris Cart to Doorstep

Integrated last-mile TMS with SAP Hybris e-commerce

Orders placed on LuLu's e-commerce portal needed to become delivery routes with no manual step in between. I integrated last-mile TMS with SAP Hybris, converted customer addresses to lat/long, and built area-based route plans against the pincode zones LuLu's team defined — dispatched to the delivery slots customers actually picked. Result: 90% on-time delivery.

WAYPOINT · BOM, INDIA · MIDDLE-MILE

Teaching a Load Planner to Think in Foam

Designed SAP-to-TMS API architecture + AI Load Planning Engine

Mattresses compress. Most load-planning logic doesn't know that. I designed the API structure pulling Sales Orders with SKU dimensions from SAP into the TMS, then built an AI-based Load Planning Engine that accounted for compressible-material behavior to maximize fill on 20/40 MT vehicles moving manufacturing-to-warehouse. This became the template — the Load & Route Planning products later rolled out to 10+ clients, cutting planning time 30% across the board.

WAYPOINT · BOM, INDIA · BUSINESS SIDE

Four Marketplaces, One Inventory Truth

Administered WMS/OMS/TMS; implemented D365 F&O P2P/O2C (business side)

On the business side for the first time, the job flipped: instead of building the platform, I owned making it work. The sharpest problem — four marketplaces (FirstCry, Myntra, Flipkart, Amazon), each with its own view of stock. I architected a real-time Seller Portal → OMS → WMS sync with dedicated zones per marketplace, and the stock-out errors from inventory mismatch disappeared. Same-Day/Next-Day Delivery followed, mapped straight off the backend pincode data.

WAYPOINT · DXB, UAE

Ninety-Nine Percent, Two Hours

Vendor selection, logistics integration, warehouse design

A Dubai marketplace wanted 2-hour delivery without yet knowing which WMS would run it. I ran the vendor selection and RFP process that landed on Unicommerce, integrated Postaplus and Noon Express as logistics partners, and designed the warehouse's inbound/outbound operations from the floor plan up. Result: 99% order fulfilment on the new on-demand model, plus a 5-month premium rebrand delivered end-to-end, including UAT.
