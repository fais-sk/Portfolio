Manufacturing · Middle Mile

Starship / Kurl-on

File No. 01 / 06

SAP-integrated TMS implementation for pan-India middle-mile movement with AI load planning, route planning, mobile POD and Control Tower visibility.

SectorManufacturing
ScopePan-India Middle-Mile Network
PlatformWebXpress TMS + SAP ERP

01 Business Challenge

Transportation planning was manual, vehicle selection was manual, load planning relied on spreadsheets, vehicle utilization was poor, shipment visibility was limited and POD handling was manual.

02 Solution Delivered

Implemented WebXpress TMS integrated with SAP ERP. Sales orders flowed into TMS with SKU dimensions, load planning used vehicle master data and compressibility logic, and dispatch execution connected to mobile POD and Control Tower visibility.

03 My Role

  • Led business workshops with SCM and logistics stakeholders.
  • Designed SAP-to-TMS API field mapping.
  • Configured vehicle masters, load planning rules and dispatch workflows.
  • Led UAT, user training, go-live and hypercare.

04 Business Impact

  • Automated order-to-dispatch flow.
  • Improved load planning and vehicle utilization logic.
  • Enabled real-time vehicle visibility using mobile GPS.
  • Digitized POD capture and delivery confirmation.
